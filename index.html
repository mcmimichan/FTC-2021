Finance

How much does FTC cost?

Early Registration, due by October 20th, will cost $165 per member. After that date, the registration price will rise to $190 per member. Registration closes on October 27th.

Are meals included in the price?

Yes, attendees will be provided breakfast, lunch, and dinner on Saturday and breakfast on Sunday.

Can my club sell merchandise/fundraise at FTC?

Unfortunately, there are no sales allowed on-site at FTC. However, you are allowed to have a pre-sale form prior and have attendees pick up items on-site. Keep in mind no transactions are allowed at FTC. Make sure to fill out a Club Sales Contract, which your treasurer should have access to. Contact our District Treasurer, Joyce Wu, for more information.

SAA

What is an SAA?

SAA stands for Sergeant-At-Arms. SAAs are volunteers responsible for knowing and enforcing the Code of Conduct and the Camp Cedar Crest Guidelines. They make sure all attendees abide by the rules at all times, ensure a safe environment for attendees, and offer assistance to members in any way that they can!

How many SAA's does a club need?

Each club with at least four members in attendance will be expected to register a minimum of one SAAs, plus an additional SAA for every five members that attend. Review the SAA handbook for more detailed information.
